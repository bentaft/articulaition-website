How do I get started with Articulaition?

Getting Started

Getting started with Articulaition is simple and takes just a few minutes:

  • Sign up for a free account - no credit card required
  • Complete your first speech analysis by recording a 1-2 minute sample
  • Review your personalized Deep Analysis report with insights on pace, tone, clarity, and more
  • Start practicing with AI conversations tailored to your specific needs
  • Track your progress over time with detailed analytics

How does Articulaition protect my privacy and data?

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our top priority. Here's how we protect your data:

  • All audio recordings are encrypted end-to-end and stored securely
  • You have full control over your data and can delete recordings at any time
  • We never share your personal information or recordings with third parties
  • Audio analysis is performed using secure, isolated processing
  • We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations
