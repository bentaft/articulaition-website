Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Articulaition different?

We focus on real-world communication improvement, not just generic speech training. Our AI analyzes your actual speaking patterns and provides specific, actionable insights you can apply immediately.

Can I change plans anytime?

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle, and we'll never lock you into a long-term commitment.

What's included in the Improvement Hub?

The Improvement Hub is our premium training space with interactive drills, situational practice scenarios, conversation simulators, and progress tracking tools. It's exclusively available to Max Growth members.

How do Expert Review Sessions work?

Each week, Max Growth members can submit one session for human expert review. Our communication coaches provide personalized feedback within 48 hours, focusing on specific areas for improvement.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All new users get access to basic analysis features to try our platform. You can upgrade to Essential or Max Growth at any time to unlock more advanced features and unlimited access.

What about privacy and data security?

Your privacy is paramount. All audio recordings are encrypted and automatically deleted after analysis. We never share your data with third parties, and you can delete your account and all associated data at any time.
